Our Services

End-to-end steel supply chain solutions from Shanghai to your port.

What We Offer

Four Core Service Pillars

🏭

Warehousing

Strategic stockyards near Shanghai port hold ready-to-ship inventory of pipe, plate and bar. We offer bonded warehousing, inventory reporting and just-in-time release to reduce your capital tied up in stock.

🌐

Procurement

Our purchasing team sources directly from major Chinese steel mills and qualified secondary producers. We negotiate competitive pricing, verify mill certificates and manage contracts so you receive the right material on time.

⚙️

Processing

Cut-to-length, sawing, threading, beveling, grinding, polishing, heat treatment and surface coating. We match processing capabilities to your drawings and tolerances, minimizing work at your facility.

🚢

Logistics

Full export logistics including container loading, lashing, documentation, customs clearance and sea freight booking. We support FOB, CIF, EXW and DDP terms with optional GPS tracking.

Made to Your Specification

Whether you need flame-cut plate profiles, precision-ground bars or beveled pipe ends, Shengxi Steel can coordinate processing before shipment.

  • Sawing and shearing
  • Threading, grooving and beveling
  • Grinding, polishing and peeling
  • Heat treatment (normalizing, quenching & tempering)
