About Shengxi Steel

Your Reliable Steel Supply Chain Partner in East China

Shanghai Shengxi Iron and Steel Group Co., Ltd. is headquartered in Shanghai and integrates warehousing, procurement, processing and logistics. With more than 20 years of experience, we supply pipe, plate, round steel, stainless steel, section steel and building materials to machinery, automotive, energy, chemical, electric power, military, aerospace and construction industries worldwide.

Steel Plate

Hot-rolled carbon and low-alloy steel plates supplied in commercial, structural and pressure-vessel grades. Common specifications include ASTM A36, A572, A516, EN 10025, JIS G3101 and GB/T1591.

  • Thickness from 1.5 mm to 300 mm
  • Cut-to-size, edge milling and surface treatment
  • Impact-tested and normalized options

Steel Rods & Bars

Carbon, alloy, bearing and cold-drawn bright bars for forging, machining and structural use. Grades cover ASTM 1045, 4140, 4340, EN 10083, JIS G4105 and DIN 1.7225.

  • Round, square and hexagonal profiles
  • Ground, polished and peeled finishes
  • Precision cutting and chamfering
