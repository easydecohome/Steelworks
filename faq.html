Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about products, quality, shipping and ordering.

Our steel complies with international standards including ISO 9001 (Quality Management), ASTM, EN, JIS and GB material standards. Third-party inspection by SGS, BV or TÜV is available on request.
We apply anti-rust coating and waterproof packaging, use containerized shipping (20ft/40ft, FCL/LCL) and can provide real-time tracking for sensitive shipments. Mandatory inspection photos and videos are taken before loading.
We support FOB (Free On Board), CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight), EXW (Ex Works) and DDP/DAP for bulk orders. Let us know your preferred Incoterm when requesting a quote.
Standard products: 7–15 days after deposit. Custom orders: 15–45 days depending on complexity. Sea shipping typically takes 15–40 days depending on the destination port.
Yes. We offer free small samples (customer pays shipping) and full-sample batches where the cost can be credited against future orders.
We accept T/T (bank transfer, 30% deposit, 70% before shipment), irrevocable L/C at sight and flexible terms for long-term partners.
All shipments are inspected before loading with photo/video proof and insurance coverage. We commit to a 24-hour response for claims supported by documented evidence.
We have delivered to North America, Europe, Africa, the Middle East and Southeast Asia, complying with local import regulations such as CE, SONCAP and SASO where required.
Yes. We provide pre-shipment documentation (commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin), HS code classification support and partnerships with local brokers in key markets.
