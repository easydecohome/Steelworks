Who We Are

Shanghai Shengxi Iron and Steel Group Co., Ltd. is headquartered in Shanghai, China. We are a leading steel supply chain service provider in East China, integrating warehousing, procurement, processing and logistics. Our annual sales exceed 800,000 tons covering pipe, plate, round steel, stainless steel, section steel and building materials.

Our materials are widely used in machinery, automotive, energy and chemical industries, electric power, military, aerospace and construction. With more than 20 years of experience, we have built a mature supply chain management system and have been recognized as a competitive modern enterprise in the East China steel industry.
